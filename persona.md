# 👥 Vigilant AI: User Personas

### 1. Mama Mboga (The Micro-Entrepreneur)
- **Pain:** Receives "SMS Phishing" daily. Lost KES 10k to a fake reversal scam in Dec 2025.
- **Solution:** Vigilant AI flags the "urgent" tone of the SMS instantly.

### 2. Juma (The Boda Boda Rider)
- **Pain:** Targeted by "AI Voice Tricks" (Vishing) where scammers mimic his boss.
- **Solution:** Threat maps warn him of high-risk callers in his area.
