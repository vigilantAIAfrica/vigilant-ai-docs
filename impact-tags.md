# Vigilant AI — SDG Feature Tag Mapping

This document connects specific technical features of the Vigilant AI engine to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

### 1️⃣ M-Pesa Scam Rule Engine
*   **Feature:** Pattern matching for fraudulent "Transaction Received" SMS.
*   **Impact Tag:** Financial Loss Prevention → **SDG 8**
*   **Narrative:** A Boda Boda rider in Nairobi receives a fake KES 2,000 transfer SMS. Vigilant AI flags the message as "High Risk." The rider identifies the scam, saves his daily earnings, and maintains his ability to support his family. 
*   **Metric:** Directly protects individual daily income.

### 2️⃣ Predatory Loan App Detection
*   **Feature:** Database of known malicious lending URLs.
*   **Impact Tag:** Vulnerable User Protection → **SDG 16**
*   **Narrative:** A small-scale farmer is targeted by a "Quick Cash" link that harvests contacts for digital harassment. Vigilant AI blocks the URL, preventing psychological distress and financial extortion.
*   **Metric:** Increases institutional trust in digital financial systems.

### 3️⃣ African-First "Shield" Infrastructure
*   **Feature:** Offline-first rule processing for low-data environments.
*   **Impact Tag:** Resilient Tech Infrastructure → **SDG 9**
*   **Narrative:** By providing a security layer that works on 3G/Edge networks, Vigilant AI ensures that even "last-mile" users have the same protection as those in urban hubs.
*   **Metric:** Modernizes regional tech infrastructure for financial safety.
